About Us

Welcome to EcoSense! This project was developed during a 24-hour hackathon by a dedicated team consisting of Rishi Golla, Amber Hasan, Dana Ibrahim, and Caden Cochran. Our mission is to leverage technology to promote energy efficiency and sustainability in buildings. EcoSense analyzes real-time energy consumption data to identify patterns and provides actionable insights to optimize energy usage. By empowering organizations and individuals to reduce waste, lower costs, and minimize environmental impact, EcoSense contributes to a greener and more sustainable future.
